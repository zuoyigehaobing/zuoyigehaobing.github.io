About Me

Hello and welcome :)!

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in computer science with a focus in computer vision at the University of Toronto. To better understand the underlying principles behind computer vision, I also chose mathematics as a minor. After finishing my third year, I worked as a computer vision research intern in Epson Canada from Aug 2018 to Aug 2019.

I am interested in finding more interpretable computer vision models through a reasonable volume of training samples so that computers can observe and learn from the world in real-time, just like human beings.
