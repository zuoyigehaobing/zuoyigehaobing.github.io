About Me

I'm a first-year MS student at the Computer Science and Engineer Department, the University of Michigan (GO BLUE!). Prior to that, I completed my bachelor's degree at the University of Toronto where I majored in computer science with a focus on computer vision. To better understand the underlying principles behind computer vision, I also took mathematics as a minor. After finishing my third year, I worked as a computer vision research intern in the interactive projector team at Epson Canada from Aug 2018 to Aug 2019。


I am interested in finding more interpretable computer vision models through a reasonable volume of training samples so that computers can observe and learn from the world in real-time, just like human beings.
